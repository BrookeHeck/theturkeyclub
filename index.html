About the Turkey Club

The Turkey Club was founded in 1978 by one big-hearted person with a spare turkey. Since 1978 many other big-hearted people have joined our ranks helping us with both their wallets and their time...supporting our fundraising events which helps us to purchase turkeys. In 2022, The Turkey Club was able to donate 1500 turkeys to 42 different meal programs and food pantries in Milwaukee, Waukesha, Dodge, Jefferson, Ozaukee and Washington counties.
